Requirement Analysis is basically a process of aquiring all information about the resources required to build a software product, it is analyzing, describing software requirements so that requirements that are genuine and needed can be fulfilled to address a specific problem.


Design & Planning

Once we have a clear understanding of project objectives as per the needs, then here comes the planning phase, This includes defining project scope, timelines, milestones, & budget. After careful planning of these variables we then begin designing phase of the product, aesthetics & such UI/UX Design are included in the process


Development

At This Phase the design is brought to life with skill and precision, it is the coding phase where proper code is written using best coding practices. A given design is transformed into an excellent product by taking care of all the functionalities and logic, while development the major focus is on functionality, scalability & Security.


Revision

Souce Code and Design Principles are also revised for better precision and accuracy for the desired outcome intended out of the software product. Different Functionalites of the software product are also taken care and looked after as a revision to offer better quality.
